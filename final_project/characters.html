the trio

chiikawa (ちいかわ)

chiikawa is displayed to be emotionally sensitive, soft-hearted, and physically weak. despite this, they exhibit selfless behavior towards their close friends hachiware and usagi. unlike their best friend, chiikawa struggles with speech.

hachiware (ハチワレ)

hachiware is known for their optimistic attitude and outstanding work ethic. it is also displayed that hachiware is notably the most mature of the three, taking accountability for their mistakes. hachiware is the only one out of the trio to speak fluently.

usagi (うさぎ)

usagi is portrayed as the energetic one of the trio, often seen teasing their friends and making jokes. despite their playful nature, usagi is the most fearless of the three. similar to chiikawa, usagi can only communicate by making their signature sounds.

the other friends

momonga (モモンガ)

momonga is a secondary character, often seen demanding yummy food and attention from others. their nature consists of trying to act cute, and causing trouble for other characters. it is implied through the storyline that a monster called dekatsuyo took over the body of the real momonga.

kurimanju (くりまんじゅう)

kurimanju is a secondary character frequently seen with the main trio and his close friend shisa. he is portrayed as a Japanese middle-aged man, often seen eating or drinking alcohol.

shisa (シーサー)

shisa takes after a traditional artifact from Okinawan mythology, sharing the same name. shisa currently works at rou ramen shop with ramen yoroi-san, and is portrayed as a diligent character. she is currently seeking to receive her alcohol license.

rakko (ラッコ)

rakko is a strong sea otter often seen assisting chiikawa and their friends by providing basic combat tips to fight monsters. he frequently visits the cafe to indulge in a sweet treat after a heavy battle.

pochette/labor/ramen yoroi-san

being the only humans in the chiikawa-land, the yoroi-sans seem to be quite friendly with the other creatures. they each have distinguishable names and characteristics based on their occupation.

chikabu/kabutomushi (ちいかぶ / カブトムシ)

chiikabu is a secondary character and former companion of chiikawa. it's portrayed that he has an appearance resembling a Japanese rhinoceros beetle, and is stated to be squishy while smelling like a sweet omelette roll.
