what is chiikawa?

chiikawa (ちいかわ); short for nanka chiisakute kawaii yatsu (なんか小さくてかわいいやつ, translates to something small and cute), is a Japanese manga and anime series by Nagano. the main theme of chiikawa is showcasing the daily lives and interactions of cute creatures and animal-like characters. while the general plot of the series has yet to be revealed by Nagano, people have come up with various theories about the plot.
