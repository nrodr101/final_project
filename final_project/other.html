who is Nagano?

Nagano is a female Japanese illustrator and manga artist specializing in various successful works. she is the illustrator and creator of chiikawa, and another franchise known as joke bear. Nagano states she has loved drawing for as long as she can remember, but no longer remembers why she started drawing in the first place. she has also stated that she admires the ruthlessness of living things, which plays a major significance in the plot of the chiikawa series.

Nagano's work

joke bear

in 2014, nagano attempted to create a LINE stamp that implemented her own jokes while placing a less conspicuous character along with it, which resulted in the development of joke bear. joke bear is a plain white bear with round ears with an undeclared gender. joke bear became a popular mascot in asia, and is widly known as 농담곰 (NONGDAMGOM) in korea.

chiikawa

chiikawa is one of nagano's popularized works, gaining millions of fans across the world. nagano has successfully launched chiikawa as not only a manga series, but an anime series as well. chiikawa was serialized on twitter in 2020, and later physically published in 2021 by kodansha. nagano has reached over 1 million followers on X (formerly twitter). an official chiikawa merchandise shop was originally launched in Japan, but later allowed their services to overseas customers.
