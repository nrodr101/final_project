chiikawa manga

the chiikawa manga was originally released by Nagano via twitter on january 2020, originally uploaded in short segments before being published as physical tankōbon volumes by kodansha. the chiikawa manga series displays chiikawa's every day life along with their interactions with other characters. there are currently seven tankōbon volumes. **note: tankōbon = "independent/standalone book" refers to a collection of manga that was previously uploaded in a serialized format

chiikawa anime

the chiikawa anime was originally announced on october 28, 2021, but wasn't premiered until april 4th, 2022. similar to the chiikawa manga, certain chapters have been animated, voice acted, and compiled into short episodes. as of late, there have been 256 english subtitled episodes but a three month hiatus was announced on march 21, 2025.

chiikawa pocket

on march 27, 2025, chiikawa pocket was the first mobile app developed by Applibot, Inc. based on the Japanese manga series chiikawa by Nagano. chiikawa pocket focuses on bringing the world of chiikawa to life. the app consists of various interactive game components such as battling monsters, weeding, and personalizing your own chiikawa world.
